We are experienced in the treatment and management of many retinal diseases including Age Related Macular Degeneration, Diabetic Retinopathy, Retinitis Pigmentosa and other inherited retinal conditions. These services are directed by Dr. Nkiruka Chinwe Nwachukwu who has worked at the forefront of modern eye care for close to 30 years, well vast in delivering the latest technology, procedures, and a 5-star patient service.

Directors Directors

Dr. Nkiruka Chinwe Nwachukwu OD MPH FAAO FNOA.
CEO

Dr Nkiruka is currently the C.E.O of Sheerah Vision Eye Clinic. an Independent Optometric Practice and Sheerah Ophthalmic Lens Surfacing laboratory an Ultramodern Lens manufacturing and Glazing laboratory with headquarters aptly located in Owerri, Imo State. She is a successful Public Health practitioner who has worked extensively on Malaria and HIV/AIDS control via advocacies to end stigmatization. She bagged her OD at Abia state University in 1993 and a Master of Science in Public Health at the Walden University United States of America. A detailed list of Dr Nkiru's personal attributes would be near impossible as she is so endowed and ably manifests courage, passion, building organisational skills, boundless energy and enthusiasm for her many projects and a firm believer in people and possibilities. Her energy is infectious and coupled with her admirable people's skills, she is able to build teams from scratch and carry them along to great successful outcomes. For Nkiru, time is no constraint as she finds extra hours to indulge her hobbies of cake making, photography, travel and an unbelievable zest for life as she drives her passion for preventive health with so much zeal organising health, fitness and ‘walk the talk’ events. Just a sneak peak at some of her formal engagements: Dr Nkiruka Chinwe Nwachukwu: Optometrist Public Health Practitioner Fellow American Academy of Optometry Fellow Nigerian Optometric Association Member British Contact Lens Association Member American Public Health Association Member Tear Film and Ocular Surface Society Member Women of Vision International Member and former chairperson Women Optometrist in Nigeria Imo chapter Immediate past Global President Federal Government Girls College Owerri Old Girls' Association Dr. Nkiruka Nwachukwu has worn numerous awards.
